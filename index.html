Why do Call it Cricket?

In the earliest definite reference to the sport in 1598 it is called creckett. One possible source for the name is the Old English cricc or cryce meaning a crutch or staff. In Samuel Johnson's Dictionary, he derived cricket from "cryce, Saxon, a stick".(courtesy Wikipedia)



Format of Cricket

There are three formats in cricket.

  • the test match which is longest format and its play in 5days.
  • second format call one day match in which each team play 50overs .
  • the third and most famous format this days call T20 short format each team play 20overs.
